WordString

Hi! I'm Stringly. Let's stitch some words together!

How to Play:

  1. Spell a new word using the last letter of the previous word.
  2. Spell longer words to score higher points!
    3L = 1pt 4L = 3pt 5L = 5pt 6L = 9pt 7L = 15pt

📲 Best experience: add WordString to your home screen to play full-screen and offline, just like a real app.

Instructions

Build words horizontally using the last letter of the vertical starter word and the 6 thread spools below.

Rules:

  • Must use at least 2 blocks.
  • Max word length is 7 letters.
  • Words of 5+ letters add +15s.
  • Undo individual letters by tapping them on the hoop!
