WordString

Hi! I'm Stringly. Let's stitch some words together!

How to Play:

  1. You start with a vertical starter word and 6 thread spool letter blocks.
  2. Spell a new word horizontally starting with the last letter of the starter word.
  3. You must use 2 or more of the thread spools!
  4. Spools are fixed during a turn, allowing you to easily unscramble 7-letter words!
  5. Longer words score much higher points:
    3L = 1pt 4L = 3pt 5L = 5pt 6L = 9pt 7L = 15pt
  6. Start with 1 minute. Words of 5 or more letters add +15 seconds!

📦 Experience is better when downloaded as an app! Play offline with full performance.

Instructions

Build words horizontally using the last letter of the vertical starter word and the 6 thread spools below.

Rules:

  • Must use at least 2 blocks.
  • Max word length is 7 letters.
  • Words of 5+ letters add +15s.
  • Undo individual letters by tapping them on the hoop!
